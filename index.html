Our Story

A Taste of Georgia
in Brooklyn

Nestled in the heart of Brooklyn, Saamo Cafe brings the warmth and richness of traditional Georgian hospitality to New York City. Every dish tells a story — shaped by centuries of culinary tradition, family recipes, and the vibrant flavors of the Caucasus.

From hand-folded Khinkali to golden Khachapuri fresh from the tandoor, our kitchen honors the soul of Georgian cooking with the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques passed down through generations.
